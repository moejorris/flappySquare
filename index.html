Flappy Bird

This is an arcade-style play until you lose game. Make the bird flap its wings to ascend.
Avoid the pipes and collect the coins in between to score! Yellow coins give you 10 points, orange gives you 25, and purple gives you 50.
Falling below or above the screen as well as hitting a pipe will make you lose a life. After losing a life you are invincible for 2 seconds. Refresh the page to play again!
